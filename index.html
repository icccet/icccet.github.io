  • Inception

    The Islamic Cultural Center (ICC) was founded in 1989 by a group of well guided muslims of College of Engineering, Trivandrum. It has now evolved into an organisation which caters the needs of muslims in the college. ICC had its humble beginnings in a rented apartment and now it has flourished into a well structured organisation occupying a three storey building.

  • Accentuate

    Since its establishment, ICC has been a platform for Muslim students to interact and share their views on various topics. It is a non aligned institution which has no affiliation with any Islamic or political organisation. ICC plans to share and propagate the teachings of the Qur'an and Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) to the student body and integrate them into a single mould.

  • Features

    ICC conducts regular Qur'an classes, professional meets, Hadith class and talks. Its own independent non-profit mess caters for the needs of hundreds of students. The ICC library equipped with several publications from around the world covers world famous debates, symposiums and programs.
