About US

The Islamic Cultural Center (ICC) was founded in 1989 by a group of well guided muslims of College of Engineering, Trivandrum.It has now evolved into an organisation which caters the needs of muslims in the college. ICC had its humble beginnings in a rented apartment and now it has flourished into a well structured organisation occupying a three storey building. ICC conducts regular Qur'an classes, professional meets, Hadith class etc and also has its own independent mess. It has a library which has several publications from around the world and also a multimedia section which covers world famous debates, symposiums and programs. Since its establishment, ICC has been a platform for Muslim students to interact and share their views on various topics. It is a non aligned institution which has no affiliation with any Islamic or political organisation. ICC plans to share and propagate the teachings of the Qur'an and Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) to the student body and integrate them into a single mould.
